Spotlight on Jat Talent

The Jat community has produced some of Bollywood's most iconic stars. From legendary actors to rising talents, Jats have left an indelible mark on Indian cinema.

Dara Singh

Legendary wrestler and actor known for his roles in classic films and as Hanuman in Ramayan.

Dharmendra

One of Bollywood's most beloved stars with a career spanning over 5 decades.

Sunny Deol

Action superstar known for iconic roles in films like Ghayal and Gadar.

Bobby Deol

Versatile actor known for his roles in romantic and action films.

Randeep Hooda

Intense performer known for his method acting in films like Sarbjit.

Mallika Sherawat

Bold and beautiful actress who redefined female roles in Bollywood.

Jimmy Shergill

Versatile actor excelling in both mainstream and parallel cinema.

Chitrangada Singh

Elegant actress known for her performances in films like Hazaaron Khwaishein Aisi.
